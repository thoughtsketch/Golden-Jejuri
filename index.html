Golden Jejuri: Pune's Next Growth Corridor


Golden Jejuri is a visionary real estate initiative in Jejuri, Pune, offering premium Non-Agricultural (NA) plots. Our ready-to-build plots are designed to cater to residential and commercial needs with top-tier infrastructure and a blend of natural and urban living. The location boasts a rich cultural heritage, with proximity to the Khandoba Temple and Morgaon Ganpati Temple, along with scenic beauty offering stunning views of the Nazare Dam and the surrounding mountains.
